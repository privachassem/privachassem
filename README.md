### 🔬 About me

I'm a cybersecurity researcher (PhD, University of Dschang, Cameroon) working at the intersection of **game theory, cyber deception, and the security of cyber-physical and automotive systems**.

My doctoral research designs **multi-layered defense and cyber-deception strategies for connected vehicles**, modeling the attacker–defender interaction with **Bayesian, signaling, and Stackelberg games** — defenses that don't just detect intrusions, but actively mislead and deter remote attackers across a vehicle's interconnected network domains.

I've collaborated with the **US Army Research Laboratory (DEVCOM ARL)**, the **US Air Force Research Laboratory (AFRL)**, and the **University of Southampton (UK)**, with peer-reviewed work in *IEEE Access*, *Elsevier Computers & Security*, and *IEEE ICNC*.

- 🧠 **Research interests:** game theory for security · cyber deception · CPS / automotive security · multi-layer defense · intrusion detection · machine & deep learning · optimization
- 🎓 **Background:** PhD Computer Science · MSc Information Security & AI · BSc Pure Mathematics & Optimization
- 🏅 **IEEE Member** · Elsevier journal reviewer · ARL doctoral fellow
- 🌍 Based in Cameroon · open to research collaboration and co-authorship
- 📫 Reach me: **priva.chassem@univ-dschang.org**
